### Textbooks

**1. Digital Design**<br/>
BY: Morris Mano and Michael Ciletti - Chapter 5 (Sequential Logic)<br/>
_Comprehensive coverage of flip-flops, registers, counters, and sequential circuit design._

**2. Digital Logic and Computer Design**<br/>
BY: Morris Mano - Chapter 5 & 6<br/>
_Fundamental concepts of sequential logic design including registers, shift registers, and memory elements._

**3. Digital Electronics: Principles, Devices and Applications**<br/>
BY: Anil K. Maini - Chapter 7 & 8<br/>
_Practical approach to sequential circuit design with detailed analysis of register circuits and applications._

**4. Digital Systems: Principles and Applications**<br/>
BY: Ronald J. Tocci, Neal S. Widmer, and Gregory L. Moss - Chapter 7 & 8<br/>
_Excellent coverage of flip-flops, registers, counters, and memory systems implementation._

**5. Computer Organization and Design**<br/>
BY: David A. Patterson and John L. Hennessy - Chapter 4<br/>
_Hardware perspective on processor registers, cache memory, and data storage systems._
